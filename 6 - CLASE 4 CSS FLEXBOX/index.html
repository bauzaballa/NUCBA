EC is a sensorial feast of honest food that combines contemporary stimulations to create thematic popups in Berlin.

Each occasion uses literary, cinematographic, philosophical and cultural influences and offers a limited number of guests a revealing experience. ​In addition to the established schedule, EC generates customized episodes for specific clients and brands; conceptual development, art direction, design dish, hosting and service are artfully delineated. Just as the exquisite Cadaver of Éluard, Breton and Tzarae created ways to extract many more from an image, Exquisite Corpse makes it possible to make of a culinary encounter an event of aesthetic fruition.

​ EC produces events, food installations, caterings, tailor made dining occasions, thematic retreats, special experience boxes. EC brings almost every (food) fantasy to life.
